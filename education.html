3. SMK TUN SHARIFAH RODZIAH



I started my secondary school in 2013 until 2017. at that time the space in my school was not enough to place all the students in one session. So my school has divided into two sessions namely morning session and afternoon session. for form one and two students, we were placed in the afternoon session where the earliest class started at 12.00 noon. While for form 3,4, and 5 students they are placed in the morning session. high school was so much fun for me because this is where I started making new friends and creating memories with friends. I attended religious classes from form one to form 5 where the addition of subjects such as Arabic, Al-Quran Skills Class, Quran and Sunnah Education as well as Shariah Education. For me, the religious stream is quite challenging because its students have to train their brains to memorize. While taking SPM, I was a bit nervous about the Arabic subject because I was a bit weak in this mastery. However, after reviewing the lessons and often focusing on this class, I managed to pass in this subject.

But I am thankful that with the help of my friends and lecturers I was able to win it and get the dean award last semester. I also hope that this last semester I will also receive the dean's award.
